Technical Coaching

Focus exclusively on your technical abilities. You spend so much time on becoming the best that you can be. Something that is amazing about Synchro is that there is always room to improve. Let me help reach the next level in your synchro career. We will concentrate on three main areas of your synchro moves.

Height

Improve your ability to get as far out of the water as possible. With my proven tactics, we can accomplish new heights!

Speed

Whether you need to work on your synchronization in a technical part of your routine or you need to slow down during your figures, there are proven methods that the Olympians use, and I can teach you too.

Control

Certain muscles and hand positions are critical in accomplishing that difficult figure or move. I can help you to perfect those positions to gain the edge over the competition.
